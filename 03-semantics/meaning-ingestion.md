SMIA – Meaning Ingestion

Meaning Space (M) does not originate within SMIA.  
Meaning is supplied to the system as structured semantic input.

Sources of meaning include:  
domain ontologies  
structured data systems  
upstream semantic extraction pipelines

SMIA does not perform interpretation of raw input.  
SMIA operates on formalized meaning.

Formalized meaning is:  
explicit  
typed  
context-bounded  
constraint-compatible

Assumption

Meaning can be represented in canonical semantic form.  
If meaning cannot be formalized, it lies outside the SMIA system boundary.

Role of SMIA

SMIA does not generate decisions.  
SMIA constrains the decision space within which decisions may exist.

Meaning ingestion is an external interface.

Governance begins once meaning is formalized.
