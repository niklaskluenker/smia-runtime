SMIA – System Invariants

Invariant I – No Ungoverned Decision  
For all a ∈ D, a has been evaluated against R.

Invariant II – No Post-Hoc Governance  
Governance = f(M, R, A)  
Governance ≠ f(D)

Invariant III – Deterministic Accountability  
Identical (M, R, A) yields identical D.

Invariant IV – Constraint Completeness  
Every excluded action corresponds to an explicit violated rule.

Invariant V – Governance Closure  
D = Gov(A)

If a decision exists, governance already holds.
